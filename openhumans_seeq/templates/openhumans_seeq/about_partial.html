About this connection

This is a small "app" that connects Seeq data to Open Humans. The following information describes how this connection app will work with you and your data.

What will this do?

This app will access data from your Seeq account and transfer a copy to your Open Humans account. Access to your Seeq data is ongoing. The app will regularly update and transfer new Seeq files to your Open Humans account.

Who is running this app?

This app is jointly operated by the Open Humans and Seeq teams.

What is Open Humans?

Open Humans is a nonprofit project that has been funded by the Robert Wood Johnson Foundation and Knight Foundation. Their platform enables members to aggregate data and share data with researchers and citizen scientists.

Data is private by default in Open Humans. You choose when to share. Members can decide to make data public, or can contribute it directly (and privately) to projects created by researchers and citizen scientists.

Want to see what projects are currently inviting Seeq data? Visit our activity page in Open Humans: {{ oh_proj_page }}

Interested in working with Seeq data in Open Humans? Read more here how to retrieve public data and create projects: https://www.openhumans.org/research/

How can I deauthorize this connection?

You can deauthorization access to Seeq data by:

  1. Loading the Seeq app
  2. Opening the menu (three dots on the top right)
  3. Selecting "Manage external studies"
  4. Toggle the "Open Humans" study.
This deauthorization removes the ability to access your data in Seeq. You can reauthorize by toggling the switch again.

You can deauthorize the app for your Open Humans account by:

  1. Visiting the activity page: http://openhumans.org/activity/seeq/
  2. Selecting "Click here to remove this connection".
This deauthorization removes the ability to send messages and add data to your Open Humans account. You can reauthorize by selecting "Connect Seeq" on the activity page.

Security

This app encrypted connections (SSL/TLS) to connect to Open Humans and Seeq for account authorization and data transfer. Please report security issues privately to Seeq and Open Humans staff.
